26.03 / 15:27 International festival to be broadcast in Internet
Yekaterinburg. March 26. KAZINFORM. The ceremony of opening and closing of the international festival of children’s musical creative activity Earth is Our Common Home will be broadcast in the Internet. Children from Kazakhstan will also participate in the festival, our correspondent reports from Yekaterinburg.
Press secretary of the Sverdlovsk region Culture Ministry Yelena Oleshko informed the festival would be also transmitted on http://webtv.uran.ru.

Director of Sverdlovsk region State Children’s Philharmonic Lyudmila Skosyrskaya told the children’s group of the city of Kostanai had arrived in Yekaterinburg.
