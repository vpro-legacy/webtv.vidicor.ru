Video Conference. Results.
10/06/2004      03:00:00

Video Conference between Ekaterinburg and Mogilev was held in the conference-hall of Palace of Sports “Mogilev” at 12.45 on June, 9
 

The conference was held with the help of means of the Internet-resource http://www.webtv.uran.ru/

Besides the heads of the cities, their assistants and representatives of the Embassies, the heads of information agencies representations, representatives of teleradioncompanies, correspondents and newspaper photographers of city and republican newspapers and magazines were present at the conference as well. There were discussed a lot of questions concerning various aspects of Ekaterinburg and Mogilev relations development. All those who wish, can get acquainted with video materials of the conference, and also with video reports from the opening of the exhibition “Urals Production and Technologies for Belarus” on the site of Scientific research center of multimedia technologies IMM UrD RAS
